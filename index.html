Process

How it works

From camera to data in seconds.

1

Point your camera

Open the app and aim your Android camera at any object. The AR system detects flat surfaces and automatically locks onto the target within 1.5 meters.

2

Auto-scan begins

The scanner fires a grid of AR raycasts every 0.08 seconds, collecting up to 300 precise 3D points that map the object's surface geometry.

3

AI identifies the object

A snapshot from the AR camera is fed into the on-device EfficientDet neural network which classifies the object and returns a confidence score.

4

Get your results

Tap "Get Data" to see all measurements, AI classification, and category. View in 3D, save to device, or clear and scan the next object.
